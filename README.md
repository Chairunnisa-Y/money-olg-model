# Money in Overlapping-Generations Models

This repository contains personal lecture notes and mathematical derivations on the role of money as a medium of exchange and store of value within overlapping-generations (OLG) models.

The materials are primarily based on Heijdra (2023), Chapter 10, and focus on intertemporal consumption, liquidity constraints, and the choice between holding money and capital assets.
